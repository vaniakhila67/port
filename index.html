My Projects

Project 1: Body Mass Index Calculator

A brief description of Project 1.

The BMI Calculator is a simple web-based tool designed to help users quickly determine their Body Mass Index, a measure of body fat based on height and weight. This project was created to provide an easy-to-use interface where users can input their height and weight, instantly receive their BMI value, and get a classification (e.g., underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese) based on standard BMI categories. In addition to calculating BMI, the tool also offers personalized suggestions on food, diet, and sleep habits to help users improve or maintain their health.
